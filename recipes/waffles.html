Waffles

You likely already have all the ingredients you'll need for this waffle recipe.

If not, here's what to add to your grocery list:

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Vanilla
  • Cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat a waffle iron according to manufacturer's instructions.
  2. Whisk eggs.
  3. Spray the preheated waffle iron with nonstick spray.
  4. Pour batter onto the hot waffle iron.
  5. Wait until golden brown, and the iron stops steaming.
  6. Serve hot.
